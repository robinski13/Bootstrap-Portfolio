About Me

Chad is originally from San Antonio, Tx and is currently
residing in San Diego, California. He graduated from
Texas A&M University with a BS in Environmental
Geosciences. Shortly following graduation Chad
commissioned in the United States Naval Service as an Ensign.
He reported to Newport Ri for Supply Corps School and upon
graduating recieved orders to the USS NIMITZ homeported in
Everett, Wa. After completing a 2 and a half year tour he recieved
orders to San Diego, California where he currently works as the
Material Operation's Supervisor specializing in medical
consumables at Navy Medical Center San Diego.
